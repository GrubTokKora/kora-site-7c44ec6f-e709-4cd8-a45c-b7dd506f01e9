About us

Tomatillos is dedicated to providing local communities with authentic Mexican food using only fresh, all-natural ingredients. All our food is cooked at each individual location, without any pre-packaged ingredients or microwaves. We believe in fresh authentic flavors and ingredients cooked to serve.

Whether you are meeting friends for margaritas, grabbing lunch between classes, or planning catering for the office, we bring the same care to every plate.
