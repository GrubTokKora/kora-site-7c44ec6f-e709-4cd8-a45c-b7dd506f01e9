Authentic Mexican Food

Open since 2011, Tomatillo is a locally owned business dedicated to providing our communities with authentic Mexican food using only fresh, all-natural ingredients. All our food is cooked at each individual location, sans any pre-packaged ingredients or microwaves. We believe in fresh authentic flavors and ingredients cooked to serve! Tomatillo is keepin' it green - we use green biodegradable paper products!
